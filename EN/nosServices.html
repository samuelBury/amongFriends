Starting from fabric-sourcing, product-development, sampling, production, quality control, we work with our partner suppliers every process in order to ensure a complete customer-satisfaction, not only for the prestigious Brands we work with, but for the end-customers too.​

Partnership with factories​

Creating partnerships with suppliers across India for production, we guarantee to have a steady quality level in service, quality as well as products. Having our own office in India, allows us to deliver on time in terms of capacity and production-planning. Our majority of partner suppliers are based around Delhi which gives us a tighter control.​ Thanks to this supplier portfolios, we are flexible and able to cater to the collection & buying-needs of our customers in terms of quantity & delivery..

Fabrics Sourcing​

Having an excellent fabric sourcing team, who visits Texworld & other fairs, bringing fabric sourcing from Far-east/Europe/ India and offering a fusion of Indian savoire-faire to these exclusive fabrics.​ Simultaneously we have a rich archive of our own prints created by talented designers from France / Spain & UK on a regular basis.​

Design and Product Development

Product development team in AFC is continuously working on new development of craftsmanship, embroideries, silhouettes based on shopping trips, Fashion weeks, Fairs across the globe and research on WGSN/Style.Com.​ ​ We have British and French designers working on new developments adapting to our client's requests.​ ​ We offer newness every months based on the latest trends .​

Sampling room​

A dedicated sampling-unit, translating the latest trends in forms of garments, accessories and working closely with the brands in developing the collections.​

Quality Control​

Quality Assurance & Quality Control:​ Our Quality team works in tandem with the brands and with the factories to reduce the lead-time for technical fits and number of submissions.​


The Quality control team is on factory floor on a regular basis to ensure the Inline and final inspections are done as per the required AQL or Inspection standards of the customers.


The successful quick turn-around of orders for our​ customers are evidence of that.​
